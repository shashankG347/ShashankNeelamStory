How It Began

Written in the stars

2019 · The Beginning

A chance meeting

At a mutual friend's Diwali gathering in Delhi, Shashank and Neelam found themselves laughing at the same terrible jokes all evening. A connection sparked.

2020 · The Friendship

Pandemic & phone calls

When the world slowed down, their conversations sped up. Hours turned into nights. Friendship quietly became something much more.

2022 · The Journey

Adventures together

From the ghats of Varanasi to the beaches of Goa, they explored India and each other. Every trip made them more certain.

2024 · Forever

He asked. She said yes.

Under the stars at Udaipur's Lake Pichola, Shashank got down on one knee. Neelam's yes echoed across the water.
